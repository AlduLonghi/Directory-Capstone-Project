UniFinder works in four simple steps.

1.search

It's simple. On UniFinder homepage enter the keyword (careers, location, budgets) for finding the perfect University. Alternatively you can click on the search link on the top menu to see all Universities available.

2.locate

Get to see where the university is located by clicking on its profile. You can get locations from all over the world! Don't feel intimidated. You are a worldwide student now.

3.apply

Now it's time for you to fill out the form that the University provides. You will have to answer some questions. This is the oportunity to show them what they are missing by not having your into the program. Show your best skills, you can do it!

4.relax

Once you get accepted you can relax and start to prepare things for this life-changing experience you are about to have. It normally takes 2 to 3 months from being accepted to the starting date. Use that time to take some rest, you will need it!
